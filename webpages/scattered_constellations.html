An eventful next picture!

We noticed what appeared to be Cygnus, as well as Lyra at 10:06 P.M. You can also see parts of Draco and Pegasus here, but they're not as prominent. You can also see the very edge of Cepheus.

You can click the images to enlarge them to see the full picture since they are cropped!

Many constellations in this picture!

My partner pointed out that the constellation in the center of the picture could be Cygnus, and after further analysis it appears that it was! I noticed the shape of Lyra after staring at my Star Wheel for a few minutes, and found the other constellations after looking through the picture for a while. I was really hoping to be able to see Hercules, but unfortunately I wasn't able to get a picture.
