First picture taken

While we were looking around the sky looking for constellations to take pictures of, we noticed our first constellation: Cassiopeia.

You can click the images to enlarge them to see the full picture since they are cropped!

I underestimated how fast the Earth moves.

This was my first time observing constellations on my own, and I ended up seeing Cassiopeia and other constellations in an entirely different section of the sky just 45 minutes later.

I've always been told how fast the Earth moves, but seeing that in less than an hour was astonishing. Especially since I live in a place with a large amount of light pollution and had to travel a little bit to see the stars.
